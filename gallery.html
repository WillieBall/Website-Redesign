Exhibitions

The School of Art galleries in Green Hall and 32 Edgewood Avenue provide a year round forum for the exhibition of work by students, faculty, and special guests in the four graduate departments of the School and the undergraduate program. During exhibitions, Green Gallery (1156 Chapel Street) is open to the public daily from noon to 6 p.m. The 32 Edgewood gallery is open during limited hours for specific exhibitions and events. Information: (203) 432-2605.
