Entrance Requirements

The School of Art requires for admission a high degree of capability and commitment. Applicants must hold a bachelorâ€™s degree from an accredited college or university or a diploma from a four-year accredited professional art school. In exceptional cases and most particularly in light of the differences among educational structures and opportunities in the international art world today, the admissions committee may waive these prerequisites if other proofs of preparation and accomplishment are deemed sufficient equivalents by the committee. In either case, admission to the School of Art is on a highly selective and competitive basis.


Graphic Design Portfolio Requirements

The images will be submitted along with your application. You must upload 20 still and/or moving image files that represent your strongest work. Titles for your work should be 30 characters or fewer and descriptions should be only 1 sentence. A good amount of this work should have been made within the last few years. For still images, you can upload your images as a jpeg, png, tiff, or bmp and should be less than 16MB. Videos should be submitted as avi, flv, mp4, wmv and less than 64MB. Titles and credits for videos are not required.


Painting Portfolio Requirements

Submit 16 still or moving images made within the last 3 years. 8 of them should have been made within the last year. Do not submit your images in presentation format (PowerPoint, Google Slides, etc.) and do not include any composite images. Formats are the same as they are for graphic design portfolios.


Photography Portfolio Requirements

Submit 20 still or moving images each with a title, date of completion, materials used, and a brief description. Most of this work should've done within the last year. File formats should be the same as for graphic design formats. If you wish to upload a bigger video file, you may host it on your own website or on another file sharing platform (Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Dropbox, etc.).


Sculpture Portfolio Requirements

Submit 20 still or moving images each with a title, materials used, and a brief description. Most of this work should've done within the last year. File formats should be the same as for graphic design formats. If you wish to upload a bigger video file, you may host it on your own website or on another file sharing platform (Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Dropbox, etc.).
