Air Travel:

Tweed New Haven Airport (HVN) is the closest airport to Yale University. This airport is serviced by American Eagle (subsidiary of American Airlines) and seasonally by Shoreline Aviation. Other airports near Yale University are JFK International Airport, LaGuardia Airport, and Newark Liberty International Airport.

Trains:

There is an hourly train from the Grand Central Station in New York every day operated by Metro-North. Amtrak also has daily routes from Washinton D.C., Boston, or New York.

Undergraduate Prospective Students

Please contact the Office of the Director of Undergraduate Studies in Art at art.dus@yale.edu or 203-432-2600 to arrange in advance for an informative student-led tour of the art school at regular business hours during the school year (Monday through Friday 8:00 – 4:00 | September – May).
